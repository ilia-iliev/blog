Getting Started with Minimalist Design
2025-12-28

Why less is more, and how to apply it to your daily life and work. Minimalism isn't about deprivation — it's about intentionality. When you strip away the unnecessary, what remains has room to breathe.

In the world of design, this principle becomes especially powerful. Every element on a page should earn its place. If a border doesn't serve a purpose, remove it. If a color doesn't guide the eye, simplify it.

The same applies to our physical spaces and daily routines. Start with one drawer, one shelf, one habit. Ask yourself: does this add value, or just noise?

You'll be surprised how quickly the clarity compounds. A simpler desk leads to a simpler mind. A simpler mind leads to better work.
